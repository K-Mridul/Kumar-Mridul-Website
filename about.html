Kumar Mridul

ABOUT

Kumar Mridul aka KM is an Indian Singer, Song Writer and Record Producer. His initial exposure came through the viral success of his First Album - Mad About You (MAY) released in Dec'2023. He was the lead singer of his Music Club - Octaves in his college. Earlier, he started with singing music covers in Hindi and English. His first Original and a step into song writing was a song about his mom, entitled "Hai Naa".

Talking about his genre and style, KM is known for unorthodox style while his genre was R&B in his first album. Mostly singing love and feel based songs, his lyrics are loved and cherished over anything. The languages involved have been Hindi, English and he has done covers in Russian, Japanese and Spanish too. He talked about his devotion and respect towards Ed Sheeran and Charlie Puth that inspired him in songwriting and music production specially.
